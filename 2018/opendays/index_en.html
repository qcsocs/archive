Theme of the year: Safe world, gaming life.

As information technology develops continuously, security risks are increasing from day to day. Ranging from network, phones, to even smart home, all of them are having potential risks of being invaded, but it is actually not hard to prevent that. We will offer games this year, to help you understand concepts related to computer security in an easier way.
