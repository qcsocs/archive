President's message - 2017

5B Wang Junyao, Eugene

We have entered the Information Age in the past decade and has been embedded in all kinds of information technology – both software and hardware. It is difficult for many people to live a day without the Internet and their smartphones. Ranging from our smartphones in our pockets, Octopus card in our wallets and cellular networks to the television and desktop computers at home, all are evidences of the use of IT in our daily lives. While these devices are constantly making your life more convenient and comfortable, have you ever thought about how they actually work behind the scene? The Society of Computer Studies is therefore, aims at introducing you a range of knowledge regarding ICT and making ‘Technology, simplified’ more than just a slogan. Let’s approach ICT and build our future together.
